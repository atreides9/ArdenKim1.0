Job Hunter

A comprehensive career management platform that streamlines job searching, application tracking, and professional networking for job seekers and career changers.

Project Overview

Job Hunter is a career management platform designed to simplify the job search process for professionals. The project aimed to create an all-in-one solution that helps users organize their job applications, track progress, network effectively, and prepare for interviews through a unified, intuitive interface.

Role

UX/UI Designer

Duration

4 months

Team

1 Designer, 2 Developers, 1 Product Manager

Tools

Figma, Maze, Notion, Slack

Problem Statement

Job seekers often struggle with organizing multiple applications across different platforms, losing track of application statuses, and maintaining professional relationships. The fragmented job search experience leads to missed opportunities and increased stress during career transitions.

Solution

Created a centralized platform that integrates job searching, application tracking, and networking tools. The solution provides a clear overview of the job search journey while offering personalized recommendations and automated follow-up reminders.

Key Features

  • Unified job application dashboard with status tracking
  • AI-powered job matching and recommendations
  • Integrated networking and contact management
  • Interview scheduling and preparation tools
  • Resume and cover letter builder with templates
  • Progress analytics and career insights
  • Automated follow-up and reminder system

Design Process

The design process involved extensive user research with job seekers across different industries and experience levels. I conducted user interviews, created personas, and mapped user journeys to identify pain points. The iterative design approach included wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing to ensure the final solution met user needs effectively.

Impact

The platform helped users organize their job search more effectively, with 78% of beta users reporting improved application tracking and 65% securing interviews faster than with traditional methods. User engagement remained consistently high with average session durations exceeding 15 minutes.
